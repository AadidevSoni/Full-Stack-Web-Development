WHAT
IS CALLED ART?

Art, also called (to distinguish it from other art forms) visual art, a visual object or experience consiously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms are Painting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theatre, Film and Music . However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the liberal arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic, Rhetoric, Arithmetic, Geometry, Astronomy and Music.
